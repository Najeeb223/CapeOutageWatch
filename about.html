What is CapeOutageWatch?

CapeOutageWatch (COW) is a community-driven tool designed to provide a quick, centralized view of essential service interruptions in the Cape Town area, focusing primarily on Electrical and Water & Sanitation outages.

We fetch publicly available service alert data from the city's alerts API and present it in an easy-to-read, card-based format, allowing you to quickly check the status, location, and estimated resolution time for known issues in your neighborhood. Our goal is simple: to make finding outage information less frustrating, and provide notifications to users for outages should they decide to receive them.

**Note:** All alert data displayed here is sourced from the City of Cape Town's official channels. COW simply presents this data in a more accessible format.

For Developers & Contributors

COW is an open-source project built with modern web technologies, aiming to be a fast and reliable Progressive Web Application (PWA). We highly welcome contributions or any kind of technical advice.

PWA Explained

This application is a Progressive Web App. This means you can "install" it directly onto your mobile device or desktop from your browser, allowing it to function offline and support features like push notifications (for alerts).
